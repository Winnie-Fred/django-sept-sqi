About Us

Sugarplum Bakery is a charming, family-owned bakery located in the heart of a quaint neighborhood. Known for its warm, welcoming atmosphere and beautifully crafted pastries, Sugarplum has been a local favorite since it opened its doors in 2008.

Owner

Amelia Thompson, a passionate pastry chef who studied at Le Cordon Bleu, opened Sugarplum after spending years perfecting her signature recipes. Her dream was to create a bakery where people could find joy in every bite.

Specialties

Sugarplum Croissants: Flaky, buttery croissants filled with a delicate plum compote and dusted with powdered sugar, a nod to the bakery’s name.

Signature Sugarplum Cake: A multi-layered cake with soft plum buttercream, adorned with sugared plums and edible flowers.

Seasonal Pies: Made with fresh, local fruits, these pies change with the seasons, from tart rhubarb in spring to spiced apple in fall.

Ambiance: The bakery's interior is cozy with vintage-style decor, featuring soft pastel tones, warm wood accents, and whimsical murals of sugarplums and other sweet treats. Small, round tables with fresh flowers encourage guests to linger over coffee and pastries.

Popular Events

Sugarplum hosts monthly baking workshops, where Amelia teaches participants how to make everything from classic French pastries to rustic bread. They also offer seasonal tasting events, like their "Holiday Sugarplum Soirée," where patrons can sample an array of festive desserts.

Sustainability

The bakery prides itself on sourcing ingredients locally and reducing waste by donating unsold goods to a nearby food shelter. They've recently introduced a "bring-your-own-container" initiative for regular customers.

Motto

"A little sweetness for every moment."
